Chess+: The revenge of minimax

2013

A game of chess where you able to customize your starting pices. You can change to position or even adding pieces of other games such as shogi or custom ones. You can play against your friends online or with an AI. I started this project when I started learning about algorithms such as minimax, and naively thought that only by implementing those I would have a decent chess engine. This game was entirely done in C# and XNA.
